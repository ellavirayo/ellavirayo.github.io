Volunteer Work

My environmental and educational initiatives in student-led organizations.

2025

DECIDE Lab Geographic Information System (GIS) Training for Sustainable and Renewable Energy

TUP-T DECIDE Lab

I trained 21 undergraduate engineering students of the Decision Engineering, Criteria Integration and Design Evaluation Laboratory (DECIDE) Lab for 80 hours on fundamental GIS: obtaining datasets, projections, map creation, vector and raster analysis, and basic spatial analysis for identifying suitable site locations.

2023

Geology Summit 2023: Orogenesis

UP Geology Majors' Society

I served as the Program Director of a nationwide educational outreach to enhance geoscientific knowledge with three events: an impromptu speaking contest, a photographic essay contest, and a free online webinar. This online event reached 245 high school students and teachers across the PH.

2020-2021

Taguyod: Alalay kay Bantay

UP Geology Majors' Society

Taguyod is an outreach program aimed to provide assistance to forest rangers of the Ipo Watershed, a critical water resource for the National Capital Region. As the Creative Head, I led production of publicity materials and program branding to increase donation calls on social media. We raised P47,149.70, enabling us to provide necessities to 60 forest rangers in financial distress due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

2022

Taguyod: Tuloy ang Daloy

UP Geology Majors' Society

In an effort to continue sharing their plight in calling for environmental justice, the UP Geology Majors' Society held a second iteration of Taguyod for the 60 forest rangers of the Ipo Watershed. I served as the Technicals Focal, where I facilitated online logistics and workflows for (1) All is Not Too Well: Raging Threats Against Forest Rangers, an Alternative Classroom Learning Experience (ACLE) activity for UP Diliman students, and (2) Guardians of The Forest: An online film viewing and discussion. These activities boosted donations for Taguyod and helped us raise P28,790, allowing us to provide a new boat for our forest rangers.
